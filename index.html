EasyChair to CEUR.WS Table of Contents

EasyChair to CEUR.WS Table of Contents is a bookmarklet that aims to create the Table of Contents for a CEUR Volume.

About

Given your conference/workshop instance, head to "Status" tab, where you should have your accepted/rejected papers.
Once in there, you can click the bookmarklet, and it will fill the html page with HTML toc, ready to be pasted in CEUR's index.html.

Requirements

  • Have rights to see the status of papers of a given workshop/conference.

How to install

Drag this link EC2C and drop it into your Bookmarks bar
